About us

Your gateway to the world of elegance and luxury with the finest shoes, clothes and accessories

Algarawi Group is the fruit of the vision and ambition of Mr. Abdulwahab Algarawi , who founded it in the early 1970s while he was a first-year university student. Mr. Abdulwahab was inspired by the historic royal decree of the late King Faisal that granted only Saudi citizens the right to own businesses.
This step was aimed at involving citizens in the national development process and motivating them to rely on themselves. This inspiration prompted Mr. Algarawi to seek to build his own project, combining his wild ambition with his deep desire to be an active part of his country’s future.
